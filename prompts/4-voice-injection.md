# Step 4 — Voice Injection

**Before running:** The Reframe Test must have passed. Do not humanize a draft that hasn't passed the gate.

---

## Prompt

```
Rewrite this draft to sound like a thoughtful human writer.

DRAFT:
[PASTE COMBINED DRAFT — hook + sections 2–5]

Add:
- Subjective observations (at least 3 lines where the writer shares a personal reading: "What's strange about this is...", "I keep returning to the fact that...")
- Natural transitions — remove any transition that feels generated ("Furthermore", "Moreover", "In addition", "This highlights"). Replace with a short sentence, a question, or a fragment.
- Sentence variation — break any three consecutive sentences of similar length. At least 3 sentences should be under 8 words.
- 2+ opinions — the writer takes a position

Remove these phrases wherever found:
- "In conclusion" / "To conclude"
- "It is important to note"
- "Throughout history"
- "It is worth noting"
- "This is a testament to"
```

---

## What to do with the output

1. Save to `states/[STATE NAME]/7. Voice Draft.md` (replace existing content).
2. Immediately run Step 5 (Imperfection) on the same file — do not stop here.
