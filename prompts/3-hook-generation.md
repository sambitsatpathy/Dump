# Step 3 — Hook Generation

**Before running:** Know your anchor reframe. The hook must gesture at it without stating it.

---

## Prompt

```
Generate 10 opening hooks for [STATE NAME].

Rules:
- No "Have you ever wondered"
- No rhetorical questions as openers
- Start with a place, a contradiction, a striking image, or a person mid-action
- Under 25 words each
- Each hook must gesture at this reframe without stating it: [YOUR ANCHOR]

Vary the approach across the 10:
- 2 hooks that open with a specific place
- 2 hooks that open with a contradiction
- 2 hooks that open with a person or unnamed figure
- 2 hooks that open with a sensory image
- 2 hooks that open with a time marker ("In 1947...", "For three centuries...")
```

---

## What to do with the output

1. Save all 10 to `states/[STATE NAME]/6. Hooks.md`.
2. Mark your favourite.
3. Edit it by hand — change at least one word. The hook is yours, not the AI's.
4. The edited hook becomes the opening line of `states/[STATE NAME]/7. Voice Draft.md`.

---

## Hook Type Reference (from design spec)

Rotate across the series — don't use the same type for consecutive posts.

| Type | Pattern |
|------|--------|
| Myth | In [state], a god is still believed to walk among people. |
| Contradiction | [State] looks modern — but its identity is ancient. |
| Place | At dawn in [place], something older than history continues. |
| Lost Glory | There was a time when [state] shaped India's intellectual future. |
| Cultural Paradox | Few places balance tradition and reinvention like [state]. |
